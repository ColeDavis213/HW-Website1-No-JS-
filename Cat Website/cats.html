the history

You might be wondering by this point, why are all these cats so sad? Well I'll let you in on a secret: they aren't. See this guy up here? He's the origin to them all. That's right. All these cats are just spin-off photoshops of this one here, taking his eyes and sometimes his whole face. It's not entirely clear when the first photoshop took place, but it is confirmed that they started taking off on 4chan in 2016. From there they spread to other places such as Reddit and Instagram and by now there are thousands of variations.

Oh, and about the original cat, he's not sad either (probably). He just has something wrong with his eyes. Which makes *me* sad, personally.
